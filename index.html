Xinyue Liu

I am an applied scientist at Alexa AI, working on natural language understanding. Before that, I obtained my Ph.D. from WPI, working with Prof. Xiangnan Kong. My dissertation is on discovering and synthesizing graphs and networks from data in other modalities, such as time series and activity logs.
